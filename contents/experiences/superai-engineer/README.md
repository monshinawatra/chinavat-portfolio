description | Won a Gold Medal — one of only 10 awarded — in Thailand's flagship national AI talent program.

Super AI Engineer is AIAT's intensive program to develop the country's frontline AI engineers. Medals are decided on total score across three levels: an entry gate (academic test, hackathon, and interview); an immersive training camp with weekly hackathons on real company problems plus a 24-hour hackathon spanning five domains — NLP, data science, signal processing, IoT, and image processing; and on-site deployment at partner organizations. Competed from House Pangpuriye and finished in the national top 10.
