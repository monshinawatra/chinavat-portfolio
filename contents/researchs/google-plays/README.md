description | Scoring how useful an app-store review is — not just whether it's positive. Peer-reviewed and presented.

A transformer-based NLP study that moves beyond positive/negative sentiment to estimate how informative a Google Play review is for driving digital engagement and guiding app development. The work was accepted and presented at iSAI-NLP 2025. It connects raw user feedback to concrete product decisions, treating review text as a signal for what to build next.
