name | Gamify Habits Tracker
description | A habit tracker disguised as a monster-catching game.

Monsters spawn at random, and the only way to feed and tame them is to complete your real-world tasks. Each finished habit earns food; enough food tames the monster; and the "catch them all" collection loop keeps you coming back tomorrow. It reframes the dull discipline of habit-building as a light, rewarding game, borrowing collection and progression psychology to make consistency actually stick. All in-game assets are produced through a pixel-art generation pipeline.
